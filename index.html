Small lab. Deep questions.

Where biology, data and AI meet.

EKLAB.AI is an independent space for bold and honest ideas. We work at the interface of bioinformatics, structural biology, computational chemistry, data science, and AI.

We like small, focused projects that ask real questions and deliver clear, reproducible answers — from molecules and 3D structures to survey data and real-world datasets.

If you have an idea, even if it’s just a rough thought, feel free to contact us at eklab.ai@outlook.com.
