Support the Lab

If you value independent, small-scale work at the interface of biology, data and AI, you can support the lab through voluntary contributions, collaborations, or by simply sharing the work with others. You can donate your old peripherals—like hard drives, SSDs, RAM modules, etc.—to support our lab’s research setup.
