Services

We'll help you on your projects which may be a mix of two or more, depending on your data, structures, and questions.

Project Consulting

Help in shaping ideas, choosing methods, and planning realistic computational or data analysis workflows. Good for early-stage projects where you are not sure how to start.

Data & Structural Analysis

Analysis of simulations, docking, structural models, or experimental datasets. The output is usually a short report with key plots and a clear summary of what the data is saying.

Pipelines & Scripts

Reusable bash, Python, or R scripts to automate MD analysis, docking workflows, or data processing. Simple, commented, and designed so you can rerun them later on new data.

Scientific Writing Support

Help with methods, results, figures, and discussion sections for manuscripts, theses, or reports. The goal is clean, honest writing that matches the actual analysis.
